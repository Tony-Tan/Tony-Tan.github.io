Posted on 2020-04-21 Edited on 2020-11-08 In category Disqus:

Abstract:

In the iteration of policy evaluation and policy improvement, the policy evaluation itself has another iteration, which is employed to calculate a optimal value function of current policy. And this iteration in policy evaluation give us an inspriation to a new method.

Value Iteration

The drawback of policy iteration is that each iteration involves policy evaluation and the policy evaluation need multiple sweeps through the state set. However, according our example, gridword, in the post ‘Policy Evaluation’ we find the greedy policy does not change after the kkk steps of the iteration of policy evaluation and the iteration stoped at step mmm and k<<mk<<mk<<m. Then, the policy interation k+1,k+2,⋯ ,mk+1,k+2,\cdots,mk+1,k+2,⋯,m does not involve the policy. So we would like to truncate the iteration of policy evaluation without lossing convergence guarantees of policy iteration.

The easiest and extreme condition is that each policy evaluation contains only one iteration. And it’s called value iteration. The initial policy is a random policy:
